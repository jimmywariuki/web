Pro Knowledge
Hardware Tips & Facts

Expert hardware tips from our team — the kind of advice you'd get from trusted hardware professionals across Kenya.

🎨

Always Prime Before Painting

Primer seals porous surfaces, ensures better adhesion and improves the final colour payoff. Skipping primer can reduce paint life by up to 50%. Use a PVA primer on bare plaster walls before applying emulsion paint.

🏪 Tip from our paint experts
🏠

Why 30 Gauge is Ideal

For residential roofing, 30 gauge offers the perfect balance of durability and cost. We stock only 30 gauge iron sheets — trusted by builders across Bungoma County for reliable protection.

🏗️ Roofing best practices
🧱

Concrete Mix Ratios

For general construction: use a 1:2:4 ratio (1 part cement : 2 parts sand : 4 parts ballast) with the right water-cement ratio. Over-watering weakens your concrete significantly. Always use clean river sand.

📐 Construction fundamentals
💧

PPR Pipes vs Black Pipes

PPR (Polypropylene Random) pipes are ideal for hot & cold water supply — they don't corrode and last 50+ years. Black pipes are suited for cold water and drainage. Never use black pipes for hot water as they may deform.

🔧 Plumbing guidance
🏺

Tile Laying: Grout Matters

Always leave the correct grout gap between tiles — usually 2–3mm for floor tiles and 1.5mm for wall tiles. Too-tight joints crack when temperature changes cause expansion. Use quality tile adhesive for long-lasting results.

🏺 Tiling & finishing tips
🔐

Choosing the Right Padlock

For external gates and heavy security, use a closed-shackle padlock — the shackle is harder to cut. For indoor use, a standard padlock is sufficient. Always oil your padlocks every 6 months to prevent rust-seizing.
